What is Moody Humans?

Moody Humans is (yet another) online mood tracker.


You can use it to track your depressive symptoms, your mood and your self-esteem. Most importantly, you can easily upload your data to Open Humans.


There are reliable measurement tools for each tracking goal: Center of Epidemiologic Studies Depression Scale Revised (CESD-R) for tracking depressive symptoms, Positive and Negative Affect Schedule (PANAS) for general mood tracking and Rosenberg Self Esteem Scale (RSES) for tracking self-esteem.


Important: If you ended up here because you are concerned about your mental health or if you have the feeling that you have been unhappy for too long, please seek professional help.


Finally, please note that Moody Humans saves your data to its own servers before passing on to Open Humans.
